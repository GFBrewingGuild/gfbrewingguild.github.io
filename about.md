# About the Gluten Free Brewing Guild

The Gluten Free Brewing Guild is a community-led space for people who care about 100% naturally gluten-free beer.

We connect consumers, homebrewers, professional brewers, maltsters, suppliers, educators, and advocates who want to improve the quality, visibility, and availability of naturally gluten-free beer.

## What we mean by gluten-free beer

GFBG focuses on beer made with 100% naturally gluten-free ingredients. This includes beer brewed from grains and ingredients such as millet, buckwheat, rice, sorghum, maize / corn, quinoa, certified gluten-free oats, and other non-gluten-containing ingredients.

We do not promote gluten-removed or gluten-reduced beer made from barley, wheat, rye, or related gluten-containing grains.

## What this site is for

This site is being developed as a public resource library for the gluten-free brewing community. It will include:

* Brewing guides and process notes
* Ingredient information
* Recipe resources
* Brewery, maltster, and supplier information
* Advocacy and labeling resources
* Community documentation
