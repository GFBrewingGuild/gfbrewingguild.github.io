# Gluten Free Brewing Guild

The Gluten Free Brewing Guild is a community-led space for consumers, homebrewers, and professional brewers who are engaged or interested in 100% naturally gluten-free beer.

## About this resource

This site is being developed as a public resource library for naturally gluten-free beer and brewing. It will include guides, ingredient information, brewing process notes, recipes, brewery resources, and advocacy materials.

## Community

Join the Gluten Free Brewing Guild Discord community to connect with other GF beer consumers, homebrewers, professional brewers, maltsters, and supporters.

More resources will be added soon.
