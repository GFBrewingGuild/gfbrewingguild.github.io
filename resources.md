# Resources

This section will collect resources for 100% naturally gluten-free beer and brewing.

The goal is to make practical information easier to find, preserve, improve, and share across the GFBG community.

## Brewing

Guides and process notes for brewing with naturally gluten-free grains and adjuncts.

Planned topics include:

* Getting started with gluten-free brewing
* All-grain gluten-free brewing
* Mash schedules
* Enzymes and conversion
* Troubleshooting

## Ingredients

Information about gluten-free brewing ingredients, including grains, adjuncts, hops, yeast, enzymes, and suppliers.

Planned topics include:

* Millet
* Buckwheat
* Rice
* Sorghum
* Corn aka Maize
* Gluten-free oats
* Yeast
* Hops
* Enzymes
* Maltsters and suppliers

## Recipes

Community recipe resources, templates, and examples.

Planned topics include:

* Beginner recipes
* Recipe templates
* Style adaptations
* Process notes
* Ingredient substitutions

## Commercial brewing

Resources for professional brewers, breweries, malt houses, and other commercial partners working with 100% naturally gluten-free beer.

Planned topics include:

* Brewery directory
* Maltster directory
* Supplier information
* Commercial process notes
* Taproom and distribution resources

## Advocacy and education

Resources explaining naturally gluten-free beer, gluten-removed beer, labeling issues, and celiac-safe brewing practices.

Planned topics include:

* Naturally gluten-free vs. gluten-removed beer
* Labeling and testing
* Celiac safety
* Consumer education
* Outreach materials
